Superdeck

Overview

Superdeck is a presentation software that improves the productivity and efficiency of finance professionals.

With Superdeck, teams can create presentations with ease and effectiveness. The software offers a range of features, including the ability to perform calculations and operations, manage data, visualize data in real-time, and seamlessly update as data changes.

Link

https://superdeck.io/

My Role

Throughout the project, I have played a key role in its development, starting from the initial stages.

In my role as a UX/UI designer, I have dedicated my efforts to crafting a user-friendly interface and ensuring a smooth and intuitive user experience.

Problem

Financial sector professionals face challenges when presenting reports on an organization's financial activities.

The need to use different programs to present financial reports and the lack of real-time data updating in tables, forms and charts increases the time spent on their preparation and leads to errors.

Solution Features

Working with data

Superdeck lets you build slides that connect to data directly so your data and narrative are never out of sync.

The platform supports working with simple tables, various charts, and pivot tables, allowing for comprehensive data analysis and visualization. Moreover, visualizations update in real-time as the data changes, ensuring up-to-date and accurate reporting.

Inputs and Expressions

The platform enhances interactivity by allowing expressions and inputs to directly reference data.

These inputs and expressions update in real-time as data changes, ensuring that the content remains dynamic and relevant.

Forms

Forms play a crucial role in the financial sector by streamlining the preparation of financial reports, budgeting, and forecasting.

They offer a standardized format for presenting financial information, ensuring consistency, accuracy, and efficiency in financial management.

UI Kit

Creating a comprehensive UI kit for the application ensured consistency in design patterns, styles, and components across all screens.

This initiative saved time, enhanced collaboration across teams, streamlined the design process, and improved usability and user satisfaction.

Results

Superdeck offers comprehensive solutions to the challenges faced by financial sector professionals in presenting reports on organizational financial activities:

  • Unified Platform: Build slides directly connected to data, syncing narrative and data to minimize errors.

  • Real-time Updates: Tables, forms, and charts reflect live data for accurate reporting.

  • Interactivity: Dynamic inputs and expressions update with data changes, enhancing presentation relevance.

  • Standardized Forms: Streamline financial reporting, budgeting, and forecasting with consistent, accurate formats.
