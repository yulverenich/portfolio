Overview

Linckr is the extension designed for automating the process of finding leads in LinkedIn and saving them in your primary CRM.

With the help of Linckr extension you won't have to process every bit of information about your leads and compare it with data in your CRM, so the lead card creation can be done in just a couple of clicks.

Link

https://linckr.in/

My Role

As the lead designer for the Linker extension, I spearheaded the entire design process. This encompassed redesigning the LinkedIn extension, developing an engaging landing page, and finalizing the application for managing the extension.

Problem

The startup successfully launched its MVP, achieved promising results, and was poised for growth. However, to advance to the next stage, the following were needed:

  • Enhanced user experience for the extension

  • Team collaboration capabilities

  • A subscription management model for teams

  • Comprehensive branding

  • Development of a landing page

Solution

Extension Redesign

In addition to refreshing the extension's visual style, the structure was revamped to enhance user experience.

Input fields were organized into logical groups, prioritized based on their effective use.

After

Team Collaboration

There was a need to enable team collaboration.

To address this, we conducted research to identify key user needs, created user journeys, and developed low-fidelity wireframes. These wireframes were then tested and refined into final designs.

Payments

The payments section addresses all client concerns related to transactions.

Users can manage subscriptions, adjust the number of licenses, add payment methods, view past payments, and apply promo codes.

Branding

After conducting a market analysis and defining the core principles that reflect the product's essence and goals, I developed several key visual elements.

These included a refreshed logo, a new color palette, fonts, and illustrations. Additionally, I prepared materials to support the marketing strategy, ensuring a cohesive and compelling brand identity.

Landing Page

The primary objective of creating the landing page was to attract new clients. This goal was achieved through the effective development of the structure and content.

Results

Here are some improvements that were achieved:

  • Increased user base

  • Accelerated lead processing

  • Enabled team collaboration

  • Enhanced brand awareness
